Dhuein de badlaan ch disda ae tera face,
Main kalla hi labda haan apna space,
Raat de pehar ch jagda haan feeling cold,
Tere naal bitaye pal lagde ne so bold.
Puraani yaad nu main dilon karda haan erase,
Tere karke hi chhadta main the whole chase,
Zakhmi dil laike main turda haan on the run,
Sach dasaan tenu main mannda haan only one.
