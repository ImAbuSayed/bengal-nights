Navian rahvaan te main turda haan my way,
Tere kite vaadeyan nu bhulda haan every day,
Vafadaari yaaran di main labda haan real talk,
Tenu main dilon kad dinda haan watch me walk.
Kaali raatan vich hun jagda ni anymore,
Tute hoye dil nu main jorda haan close the door,
Bheed vich kalla main kharda haan high above,
Apni main manzil nu labda haan pure love.
