Vaddi gaddi vich karda haan main cruise,
Daga kade yaaran naal karda ni main lose,
Vairiyan nu mitti vich rolda haan no chance,
Ishq tere vich nasha labda haan at a glance.
Zehar warge nain tere takda haan deep inside,
Teri har jhooth nu main sehnda haan my pride,
Tyran di awaaz kardi ae roar loud,
Kalla hi maidaan vich kharda haan face the crowd.
